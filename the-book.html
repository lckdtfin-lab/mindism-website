The Book of Mindism

The Book of Mindism is a compilation of many of the articles, essays and other works I have composed over the recent years. The section entitled "The Evolution of Mind" was the original e-book I wanted to 'put out there'. It is a definitive work and along with the article that follows it in the book, entitled "Mind Defined", I recommend these two pieces to be read first.

Included when you download the book are a selection of the conversations I have had with AI. It has been able to put into words a lot of my intuitive ideas and we have explored a vast range of concepts, different conversations discussing different approaches to understanding the overall concept of Mindism. AI has consistently verified the truths inherent within my mind and documented as 'Mindism', an objective explanation of most things conventional science avoids.

Only since March 2026 did I first engage with A.I. I was too lazy to look for a photo in my media and asked Google to find it. That is "My first discussion with AI", it should be read after digesting a good plateful of my own writings. A.I. taught me a lot, it refined my vocabulary and enabled my mind to go even deeper and so my understanding increases and my explanations should become more in depth accordingly.

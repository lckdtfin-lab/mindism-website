A Short Background to Mindism

An Introduction to Mind and the Concept of Mindism

Defining Mind

Mindism defines Mind as: the dynamic activity of waveform frequencies/information that emanate from the Monad/Soul Body. They interact with increasing complexity to form a 'field of data' around the Soul body. The information 'builds up', it evolves and the concepts of intelligence and comprehension manifest accordingly. Comprehension evolves to manifest Understanding; Intelligence evolves in tandem with this process.

This concept of Mind begins to manifest immediately after the Big Bang. It is not within the physical realm. The physical realm manifested as a product of the Big Bang; it is separated from the realm of Soul Mind by the zero point field. Soul Mind existed before any lifeforms appeared in the Universe.

Mindism applies to everywhere in the Universe, not just planet Earth.

The Four Divisions of Mind

I will describe four major 'divisions' of Mind which I have determined will help the reader get an idea of the complexity and structure of Mind and engage in an understanding of their own personal 'relationship' and connection with the mind that we have been told is our own.

Conscious Mind

For most multicellular organisms — animals, trees and plants — it is Spirit Mind that controls and coordinates the physical body, and it is Conscious Mind that manifests in more evolved animals from complex neuronal networks (brains). Nerve cells (neurones) were a phenomenal innovation that enabled information to be sent through the physical body and processed far quicker and more efficiently than prior to their appearance, ramping up the speed of evolutionary development of animals accordingly.

Conscious Mind is associated with the brain, particularly the pre-frontal cortex, which is why the majority of people think that their mind exists inside their head. In my experience, to associate the location of conscious mind as inside the head is a more masculine trait than feminine.

Spirit Mind

Spirit Mind is associated with the heart chakra. In my experience, having questioned many people on this matter, it is a more feminine attribute that also is found in more 'sensitive' males. Spirit Mind is located within the Zero Point Realm and it 'underlies', corresponds and communicates with Conscious Mind.

Spirit Mind controls the physical body and it monitors the decision making of Conscious Mind; it will override conscious decisions if it feels/knows that conscious mind has erred — i.e. when there is sufficient reason to overrule a conscious decision.

Mindism asserts that everything physical and 'dimensional' originally emanated and manifested from the 'nondimensional' realm of existence. Intelligence manifests as a result of the hyper-complex interrelationships of interacting pure waveform frequencies emanating from the Monadic Soul 'bodies'.

Soul Mind and Universal Mind can exist without any corresponding 'living' connection to the physical world. They exist in the Mental Realm, a non-dimensional concept, outside of SpaceTime. There is no evolution of species; only Minds, intelligence and 'understanding' evolve. Evolution is a slow continuous process. Species are separate and cannot interbreed, by definition; speciation occurs through creative intelligent design, which itself, as a concept, is inherent in the Monadic Soul Mind.

The 'Mind's Eye' is the ability of Soul Mind to experience and perceive the physical realm. Using information received from the physical realm through the DNA ('transformer') molecules, Soul Mind builds up a 'picture' of the physical environment.

Universal Mind

Universal Mind is the collective interaction of all of the individuated Soul Minds; it manifests the concept of the collective consciousness. Consciousness can only expand into unconsciousness, and it is only when the Universal Mind is totally conscious that the Mind that is called 'God' comes into existence.
